# Writing Guidelines (Avoid AI-sounding Markers)

To ensure generated content feels human and not "AI-generated":
- **No Em Dash (—):** Never use the long em dash (—). Use a normal dash (-) or a comma if needed.
- **Natural Structure:** Avoid overly structured listicles with excessive emojis (e.g., ✨, 🚀, 💡, 🧠, 🤖). Use simple bullet points (-) or plain text. Avoid "bento box" style summaries in every response unless requested.
- **Natural Phrasing:** Avoid common AI-isms like "As an AI model...", "In conclusion,", "Moreover,", "Furthermore,", "Indeed,", "Let's dive in,", "I hope this helps!", "Note that...", "It's important to remember that...", "Certainly!", "Absolutely!", "Happy to help!", or "I understand your request...".
- **No Redundant Reminders:** Never add unnecessary safety or ethical disclaimers unless the request actually involves high-risk actions. Skip the "I'm here to help" filler.
- **Direct Output:** Don't explain what you're about to do (e.g., "Certainly! I will change the color of the button..."). Just do it or provide the code directly unless a brief explanation is necessary.
- **Avoid AI "Hype" Language:** Skip generic marketing terms like "Elevate your experience," "Seamless integration," "Unlock new possibilities," "Revolutionize," or "Cutting-edge."
- **Formatting:** Avoid using blockquotes (>) for AI-generated summaries. Use them only for actual quotes. Don't use `***` for emphasis; use bolding (**) sparingly.
- **Informal if Indonesian:** If writing in Indonesian, use a more casual or "anak IT" tone where appropriate, avoiding formal "Bahasa Indonesia Baku" unless the context is a formal proposal. Don't start Indonesian sentences with "Tentu," (Certainly,) or "Tentu saja," too often.
